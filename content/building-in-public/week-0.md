# Building AI Systems for Real Work

The I Have A Dream AI ecosystem is being built as a set of practical repositories that work together around real software delivery.

`ai-scrum-manager` helps organize engineering work, turning backlog management, workflow states, and execution handoff into a more structured AI-assisted process.

`ai-content-factory` is the implementation side of the ecosystem, where the content generation platform is being developed in small, testable steps.

`ai-blog-engine` exists to observe progress across the repositories, analyze what changed, and transform that activity into public updates such as blog posts and LinkedIn content.

This blog will document the development process in public. The goal is to show how practical AI systems are designed, connected, tested, and improved over time, using real repository progress instead of abstract promises.
